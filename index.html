About Us

UYA (Uyabusisa Arts and Development) is a proudly South African company rooted in handmade excellence. Founded by Zanele Dube, a textile designer and artisan since 1996, our mission is to create economic empowerment through creativity and high-quality craftsmanship.

Vision

To change lives and improve the economy by equipping creatives and entrepreneurs with valuable craft and design skills.

Mission

To advance African creativity through excellent product development, training, and sustainable handmade goods.

Values

We are committed to quality, empowerment, creativity, and cultural pride.
